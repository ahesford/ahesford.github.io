Spreading Awareness

Civil Air Patrol Information is committed to increasing the public awareness of the Civil Air Patrol. That is why we are interested in knowing when we help individuals join the program. If part of your decision to join the CAP was based on information or guestbook entries from Civil Air Patrol Information, e-mail Cadet Mulder at ofcrmulder@yahoo.com. Tell us if you want to remain anonymous, or if you would like your name displayed.
